Financial Analysis Report

Challenge Overview

This project involves a comprehensive analysis of financial news data to uncover correlations between news sentiment and stock market movements. The aim is to enhance predictive analytics capabilities for improved financial forecasting accuracy.

Business Objective

Nova Financial Solutions seeks to refine its predictive analytics by integrating financial news sentiment with stock market data. The goal is to provide more accurate forecasts and insights for better decision-making in financial markets.

Dataset Overview

The dataset consists of historical stock price data and financial news headlines. The key files are raw_analyst_ratings.csv and AAPL_historical_data.csv, which contain the news and stock price information respectively.

Competency Mapping

This week's challenge will contribute to developing expertise in Data Engineering, Financial Analytics, and Machine Learning Engineering. Tasks will involve quantitative analysis, sentiment analysis, and visualizing the relationship between news and stock movements.

Deliverables and Tasks

Task 1: Set up and manage version control using Git and GitHub.

Task 2: Conduct quantitative analysis using TA-Lib and PyNance for technical indicators.

Task 3: Analyze and visualize correlations between news sentiment and stock market movements.
